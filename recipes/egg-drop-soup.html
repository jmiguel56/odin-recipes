Egg Drop Soup Recipe

Delicious gold, the egg drop soup containing eggs, broth, and many more will surely warm you during a meal

TIME: 15-20 min

SERVINGS: 4

Ingredients

  • 3-4 eggs
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1 tbps cornstarch
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tbps soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 3/4 enoki/shittake mushrooms, optional

Steps

  1. Make the cornstarch slurry:

    2. Measure out 1/2 cup of the stock and mix with the cornstarch until dissolved.

  2. Bring the soup ingredients to a boil:

    3. Place the remaining chicken stock, mushrooms, green onions (reserving a few for garnish), ginger, soy sauce, and white pepper in a pot and bring to a boil.

    Stir in the cornstarch slurry and reduce the heat to a simmer.

  3. Stir in the beaten eggs:

    4. Slowly pour in the beaten eggs while stirring the soup slowly. The egg will spread out into ribbons.

  4. Serve:

    5. Turn off the heat and garnish with a few more chopped green onions. Serve immediately.
